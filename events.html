Events

Come out and meet the players you've gotten to know online for some IRL fun.

LAN Parties

LAN Parties

LAN parties are a great way for you to meet people to play with! Whether it's inhouse ARAMs or Ranked Duos or TFT, as long as you're here to play, you'll fit right in.

Tournaments

Tournaments

Want to put your skills to the test against other Berkeley students? We host a variety of competitive and 4fun tournaments every semester, including the Berkeley Legends Challenger Series, ARAM, Tandem, and more!

Networking with Rioters

Events with Rioters

Wondering what it's like to work at Riot? Whether you're interested in the tech operations of Riot or the more player-based projects, Riot representatives (including Berkeley alumni) are here for you to network with and get your questions answered. We also personally tour the Riot Games studio in LA every spring!

Socials & Banquets

Socials & Banquets

Berkeley Legends socials are a great way to meet fellow summoners on campus, and enjoy boba runs, board and card games, casual hangouts, and study sessions! Find friends with similar interests, and 1v9 everyone in One Night Werewolf.

Viewing Parties

Viewing Parties

Join us to watch League of Legends championships and tournaments together. Meet other Berkeley Legends members while cheering for your favorite team! Participate in group games and trivia to win prizes such as lanyards, wristbands, teemo hats, and plushies.

Trivia

Trivia

According to Gangplank, what does betrayal sting like? Take your 200IQ plays beyond the rift to dominate the competition in game-related trivia! Compete in teams for fun, glory, and prizes.
