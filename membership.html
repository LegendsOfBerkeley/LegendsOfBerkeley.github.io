Membership Program

Get the most out of Berkeley Legends by becoming a verified member.

Perks

🍃 Free tournament admission
All tournaments will be available to you free of charge! Berkeley Legends hosts a variety of tournaments throughout every semester, ranging from ARAM to TFT to BLCS and 5v5.

🍃 Member-only socials
We will be hosting a members-only barbecue as well as a banquet where we, the BL board, will provide food, games, and cheery company for all our lovely members.

🍃 Riot studio tour
In the spring, Berkeley Legends travels to LA to tour Riot HQ and chat with some Rioters! You can even (optionally) stay for a second day to watch LCS with the club.

🍃 Family system
Join a family headed by a Berkeley Legends board member and one of your fellow members! Families are a smaller groups of BL members designed to let everyone to get to know each other better within the broader League community at Berkeley. Family hangouts, League games, and friendly rivalries with other families are just a few of the events you can get involved in.

🍃 Point system
Earn points for attending Berkeley Legends events! Our most dedicated members will receive prizing as thanks for supporting us (past rewards: gaming headset, RNG team swag, and more).

🍃 Custom Discord emote
Leave a legacy as your face is immortalized in the BL Discord.
