Greetings from Berkeley Legends!

CSUA is hosting the Riot Tech Talk this year! So if you are interested in an engineering intern position at Riot, this is your chance to get more details. The event will be on October 14th, 2015 from 6 - 7pm at Blue Room, Career Center.

More about the event: Designing the infrastructure to scale a game to tens of millions of players is a hard problem. Staffing and organizing the teams to execute on that design is an equally hard problem.

Come join this talk with Engineering Manager Tyson Trautmann, to learn more about the technology and teams behind League of Legends, including both the way that the backend for the game is deployed and operated and the way that teams and organizations are structured at Riot.
