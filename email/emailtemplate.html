Greetings from Berkeley Legends!

As Season 4 has come to an end, we're excited to host Berkeley Legend's Post-Season Social on Friday Nov. 14, featuring board games, group activities, and pick-up games of League. We have rooms in Dwinelle reserved, so for those of us who have exams and projects to focus on, we also have a study hall.

We'll start the night with a boba run at ShareTea at 6:30 pm. Feel free to get food To Go (dinner / snacks) if you want to eat at the event! If you can't make it until later, please check the event page for logistics and room numbers.

This is a great opportunity to get to know the rest of the club better as well as find a team for LANB4TIME. Bring your friends, it'll be fun!

Announcements:
- If you have yet to pick up your tshirt, you will be able to pick yours up at this event.
- If you have a power strip/extension cable, we kindly ask that you please bring it with you!

See you soon,
