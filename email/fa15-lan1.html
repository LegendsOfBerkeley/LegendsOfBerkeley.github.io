[Berkeley Legends] First General Meeting - Spring 2015 [Berkeley Legends] LAN PARTY #1 - Fall 2015

Greetings from Berkeley Legends!

Midterms are over so come out and join Berkeley Legends at our bi-monthly LAN party! Unfortunately you will have to bring your own laptop, mouse, charger, and extension cord. But we will provide the space for you to socialize and play competitively amongst and/or with each other!

The event will take place on Tuesday, October 13th from 7 - 9pm at 87 Evans.

We also have a very special surprise for you! Rioters will be joining us at the LAN party. So if you have ever had questions about it is like being a Rioter, what kind of projects they work on, or how the community is like at Riot Headuarters, this is your chance to ask them! They are here to socialize and get to know the members of Berkeley Legends. These are the four Rioters who will be joining us for the event:

Tyson Trautmann, Engineering Manager
Leigh Estes, Software Engineer
Mike Matsumura, Sr. Software Engineer
Michelle Duffie, Recruiting

So come with enthusiasm to play the game, and questions to ask!

* Berkeley Legends is the official Riot supported League of Legends club at UC Berkeley.
