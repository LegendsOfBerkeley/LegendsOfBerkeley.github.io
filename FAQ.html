FAQ

Do I have to be ranked to be a part of Berkeley Legends?

Of course not! We welcome everyone who is interested in and would like to be a part of the League of Legends community at Cal. Please don't be intimidated from joining in if you are new or unranked. Joining Berkeley Legends is a great way to find others to play with.

How do I join the competitive scene at Cal?

You can contact the competitive coordinator, Patrick Visan (Discord: LegendAchiever9#6820), for more information on UC Berkeley's competitive collegiate teams and how to join. Tryouts are usually held at the beginning of the Fall semester for placement into our teams.

What exactly is this club, and what is League of Legends?

Berkeley Legends is the official Riot Games sponsored League of Legends club at Cal that hosts community events for its student members. Berkeley Legends strives to provide support and common ground for passionate gamers from all across campus to gather, foster community, and overall have a blast. We are always unleashing a variety of different events throughout the semester for awesome and intense fun. Look out for us and our upcoming events!

If you are unfamiliar about League, and want to know further about what League of Legends is all about, visit the League of Legends official website to learn more.

Is Berkeley Legends an online community, or is it an on-campus organization?

Both! We're proudly a very strong on-campus organization, and we've had tremendous success with on-campus socials and other events. We hold general meetings, social gatherings, LAN tournaments, and viewing parties, as well as online League of Legends competitions. You can also expect some new, cool and different events as well. Keep up to date with the facebook page and events site for the latest info.

Does Berkeley Legends only put on game-related events?

Not at all! We host board-game night socials, study halls, and boba runs as well. We're always open to new event suggestions, so you can expect a variety of social gatherings where you can meet and mesh with new and different Berkeley Summoners to hit up later on the rift, or just hang out with for a good time.
